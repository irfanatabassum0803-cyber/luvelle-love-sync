💖 Luvelle – AI-Powered Relationship Companion  

 Love grows when connection becomes intentional.  

Luvelle is a full-stack SaaS platform designed to help couples stay emotionally connected through adaptive daily activities, AI-powered relationship insights, and continuous feedback systems.  

Built to strengthen communication, emotional bonding, and long-term relationship wellness in a modern digital world.  

🚀 Live Demo  

🔗 https://liberal-luvelle-love-sync.base44.app  

📌 Problem Statement  

Modern relationships often struggle due to busy schedules, communication gaps, emotional misunderstandings, and lack of consistent quality time. Many couples care deeply for each other but gradually lose connection because they lack tools to nurture their bond daily.  

Traditional relationship apps focus only on chatting or reminders — but they fail to provide intelligent emotional support, adaptive engagement, and meaningful connection-building experiences.  

💡 Our Solution  

Luvelle is an AI-powered relationship intelligence platform that helps couples stay emotionally engaged through:  

- Personalized daily bonding activities  
- Emotion-aware relationship insights  
- AI conflict resolution guidance  
- Shared planning tools  
- Journaling & memories  
- Continuous emotional feedback systems  

Luvelle transforms relationships from passive connection to intentional growth.
